Who is Rufai Balogun?

Hello! Welcome to my E+ EMCDE Portfolio. I am Rufai Balogun and had a background in Meteorology and Climate Science from the Federal University of Technology, Akure, Nigeria

I have worked at the interface of Environment, Climate and Disaster Risks. I have a strong interest in Earth Observation for Sustainable Development. At the moment, I am pursuing a degree in the Copernicus Master in Digital Earth funded by the Erasmus Mundus Plus Program in conjunction with the European Commission.
